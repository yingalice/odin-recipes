Herb Cauliflower Rice

Description

Time: 20 minutes

Herb cauliflower rice is a delicious guilt-free side dish! Cauliflower is low carb, high in vitamins and fiber, and a good source of antioxidents. Cauliflower by itself is pretty boring, but kick it up a notch by adding butter, garlic, fresh herbs, and fresh lemon juice, and you get a herby flavor explosion! Then sprinkle almond slices to introduce some extra crunch and texture. Yum!

Tip: I prefer to use store bought cauliflower rice, as it was too much trouble and messy to cut it into tiny pieces. But if you do opt to make your own, you can always store extras in the freezer for 1-2 months.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 16 oz cauliflower rice
  • salt and pepper
  • 3/4 cup chopped fresh herbs like parsley, cilantro, and basil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps

  1. Stir almonds in pan over medium heat until lightly toasted, about 3 minutes. Set aside in bowl
  2. Heat butter in pan until melted and bubbling. Then stir in garlic, and cook for 30 seconds
  3. Add cauliflower rice, salt, and black pepper. Cook until rice starts to turn light brown, about 8 minutes
  4. Remove pan from heat. Stir in fresh herbs, lemon juice, and toasted almonds
