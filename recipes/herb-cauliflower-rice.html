Herb Cauliflower Rice

Description

Time: 20 minutes

Herb cauliflower rice is a delicious guilt-free side dish! Cauliflower is low carb, high in vitamins and fiber, and a good source of antioxidents. Cauliflower by itself is bland, but kick it up a notch by adding butter, garlic, fresh herbs, and lemon juice, and you get a herby flavor explosion! Then sprinkle some almond slices to introduce some extra crunch and texture. Yum!

Recipe source: https://www.inspiredtaste.net/40958/herb-cauliflower-rice/

Ingredients

Steps

  1. Stir almonds in pan over medium heat until lightly toasted, about 3 minutes. Set aside in bowl
  2. Heat butter in pan until melted and bubbling. Then stir in garlic, and cook for 30 seconds
  3. Add cauliflower rice, salt, and black pepper. Cook until rice starts to turn light brown, about 8 minutes
  4. Remove pan from heat. Stir in fresh herbs, lemon juice, and toasted almonds
