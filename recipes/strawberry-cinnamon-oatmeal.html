Strawberry Cinnamon Oatmeal

Description

Time: 3 minutes

Strawberry cinnamon oatmeal makes for a hearty breakfast, and is a great way to fuel up for the day. It only takes 3 minutes to make, and is healthy, filling, and sweet! To switch things up, you can substitute the strawberries, if you're in a peach or blueberry mood today!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • dash of salt
  • 1/2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 strawberries, sliced

Steps

  1. Combine oats, milk, and salt in a bowl. Microwave for 1.5 minutes
  2. Stir in brown sugar and cinnamon
  3. Cut strawberries into slices, and arrange in a circle on top of oats
