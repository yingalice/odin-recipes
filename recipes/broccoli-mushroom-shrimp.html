Broccoli Mushroom Shrimp

Description

Time: 25 minutes

Broccoli mushroom shrimp stir fry in a classic ginger sesame sauce is packed with veggies and proteins, providing a well-balanced meal for the family. This tasty dish is simple, but yummy, and is easily made from kitchen and pantry staples.

It is very saucy, and complements rice perfectly! The flavor is mild, and leaves you feeling good afterwards, unlike the overly salted takeout that is all too common.

Ingredients

Sauce Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch

Dish Ingredients

  • 2 - 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 thumb size ginger, grated
  • 1 lb mushroom, sliced
  • 1 lb broccoli crowns
  • 1 tsp roasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Add all sauce ingredients except cornstarch in a bowl, and stir well. Stir in cornstarch, then set aside
  2. Heat oil in pan. Add shrimp, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2 minutes, then set aside in bowl
  3. Heat oil in pan. Add onion, garlic, and ginger, stirring after each addition. Cook for 1 minute
  4. Add mushrooms. Cook for 1 minute
  5. Add broccoli crowns. Cook for 3 minutes
  6. Add sauce mixture from Step 1. Toss to coat
  7. Add shrimp from Step 2. Gently toss
  8. Add salt, pepper, and toasted sesame seeds. Toss to combine
  9. Serve over rice and enjoy
